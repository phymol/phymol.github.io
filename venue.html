Conference Venue


The conference will take place at Campus Limpertsberg of the University of Luxembourg, in the Bâtiment des Sciences (BS) building, auditorium BS 0.03. The auditorium is immediately to the right after the front entrance to the BS building.

As an aside, note that all public transportation within Luxembourg, buses, trains, and tram line, are all free and require no tickets.

Travel


We have booked rooms at the Best Western Plus on 3 Av. Victor Hugo, Limpertsberg for all of our guests. More travel information will soon follow.
